Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about ChosenX, our services, career opportunities, and how we can help your business grow.

ChosenX specializes in three core services: Retail Sales - offering direct sales and marketing by collaborating with major retailers; Brand Building - taking advantage of high consumer traffic to promote clients' products and services; and Market Expansion - providing management training programs to ensure business growth and success.

Our headquarters is located at 849 W Levoy, Salt Lake City, UT 84123. We're a young sales & marketing firm based in Salt Lake City with plans to expand to every major city in the country. Our office hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00AM to 5:00PM.

You can apply by visiting our careers page or clicking the "Apply Today!" button. We're rapidly expanding and looking for highly motivated team members for our sales and marketing teams. We offer both entry-level positions and management trainee opportunities with clear paths for advancement.

We specialize in working with Fortune 500 companies to help them develop their localized retail presence and increase brand awareness. Our established track record of success has led our clients to request expansion to major cities across the country.

We work hard, play hard, and have a dynamic team culture where we reward high achievers and seek to establish an enjoyable team environment. We believe that a satisfying career begins in a pleasant and inspiring workplace, so we strive to create an environment where individuals develop lifelong careers rather than short-term jobs.

We set clear and defined goals for promotion and educate entrepreneurial skills to team members who want to advance into leadership and management positions. We have developed a career advancement path from sales to management, with opportunities for those who are looking to work hard and advance their careers.

For our Sales Representative positions, we require 3+ years of experience in a customer-facing role, strong people skills, productivity and results-driven mindset, positivity, and a growth-oriented attitude. We're looking for leaders, motivators, and those who are eager to learn and don't like lazy people!

You can reach us by phone at (385) 218-4308, email us at contact@chosenxinc.com, or visit our office at 849 W Levoy, Salt Lake City, UT 84123. Our business hours are Monday through Friday from 9:00AM to 5:00PM. You can also use our contact form on the website.
