About ChosenX

A young sales & marketing firm in Salt Lake City with an established track record of helping Fortune 500 companies develop their localized retail presence.

Our Story

ChosenX is a young sales & marketing firm in Salt Lake City. We have had a lot of success helping Fortune 500 firms start and develop their localized retail.

With an established track record of success, our clients want us to expand to every major city in the country. This was our client's most important market to take over next. This city is one of the most rapidly rising and desirable destinations to do business.

We work hard, play hard, and have a dynamic team culture in which we reward high achievers and seek to establish an enjoyable team environment.
